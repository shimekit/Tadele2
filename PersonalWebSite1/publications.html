Genetic Diversity of Coffee ( Coffea arabica L . ) Landraces from Southern Ethiopia as Revealed by Inter Simple Sequence Repeat Marker

Despite its importance, the genetic variability of landraces Coffea arabica L. from Southern Ethiopia has not yet been examined well with molecular markers. Hence, in this study the genetic diversity of 87 coffee germiplasms from Southern Ethiopia was studied using five Inter Simple Sequence Repeats (ISSRs) primers.
